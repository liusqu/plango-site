FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Plango.

What is Plango?

Plango turns the travel screenshots you've been collecting — from social media, chats, anywhere — into a fully planned, day-by-day trip. It checks that every day actually works (opening hours, ferry schedules, travel time), then writes you a magazine-style roadbook to carry along.

How do I start a trip?

Create a trip and drop in your screenshots; Plango recognizes the places and plans around them. No screenshots? Just type the names of a couple of places you've heard about — or paste a whole guide you copied from anywhere — and Plango will turn them into real places and build from there.

Does Plango book hotels and activities for me?

Plango prepares each booking (what to book, when, and with whom) and hands you to the provider's own site or app to pay. Your payment always goes directly to the provider, and Plango never adds a service fee on top.

Is Plango free?

Plango is free to download and try. Unlocking a trip's full plan and generating its roadbook are one-time in-app purchases; current pricing is always shown in the app.

Are my screenshots and trips private?

Yes. Your content is processed only to build your own trips, is never sold, and is never used to train third-party AI models. Details in our Privacy Policy.

How do refunds work?

Purchases go through the App Store, so refunds are handled by Apple (reportaproblem.apple.com). If anything went wrong on our side, email us first — we'll help.

Something else?

Write to support@plango.one — we read everything.
