第二章 · 卡塔利娜Chapter 2 · Catalina

十四头野牛的下落Abandoned Extras and a Casino That Never Gambled

沿纪念碑旁的山路继续往内陆走，草坡上偶尔能看见一种不该出现在太平洋岛屿上的东西：美洲野牛。它们的曾祖辈是一九二四年好莱坞剧组拍西部片《消失的美国人》时运上岛的道具——十四头活牛。拍完之后，剧组算了算运费，决定不运了。Then there are the bison. Fourteen American bison were shipped to the island in 1924 for a silent Western called The Vanishing American. When the shoot wrapped, the studio did the math on ferrying fourteen large animals back across the channel, decided it was not worth the freight bill, and simply left them.

十四头牛就这么被留在了一座离大陆四十公里的岛上，像一群被辞退后找不到回家路的临时演员。一百年过去，它们繁衍到了一百五十头左右，如今受法律保护，比岛上大多数人类居民的资历都老。The bison did not vanish. They multiplied. They are the island's most photographed residents — abandoned extras who outlasted the movie, the studio, and most of the audience that might have watched it.
