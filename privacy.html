Privacy Policy

Effective: July 4, 2026 · Plango is operated by F.I.R.E holding ("we").

What we collect

Your account email; screenshots and trip content you choose to upload (processed to recognize places and build your itinerary); trip preferences you set; basic usage diagnostics. We do not sell personal data, and we do not use your content to train third-party AI models.

How content is processed

Uploaded screenshots are analyzed by AI services under contract with us solely to extract travel-related places and details for your own trips. Results are stored in your account and deleted when you delete the trip or your account.

Bookings

When you book hotels, activities or transport, you are taken to the provider's own site or app; your payment details are handled by that provider, never by us. We may earn a referral commission from providers at no extra cost to you.

Third-party services

We use Supabase (hosting & database), Apple Sign-In, Google Maps/Places, and travel partners (e.g. Expedia, Viator) for the features above. Each processes only the data needed to serve you.

Your rights

You can export or delete your data at any time from the app (Me → Account), or by emailing info@fire-holdings.com. California residents: we honor CCPA access/deletion requests at the same address.
